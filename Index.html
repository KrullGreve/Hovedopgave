Cruise Specific Services

Strategic Planning

Complex projects require strong organisation and careful planning. Every step in our delivery plans is being scrutinised to keep the time frame and ensure precision

Coordination of Specification

We break furniture & fabric packages down into categories, assign the expert and “pull” each spec sheet apart to coordinate every single detail.

Quality & Design

Cruise quality is an acquired skill that is learned through experience. To exceed quality expectations, our quality management system is an integral part of every activity and task.

Architect & Designer Support

Designing spaces, cabins and public areas is a discipline in its own league. We support you with technical shop drawings and 3D renderings so every detail can be cleared before moving forward.

Transparency

Transparency is not just a principle but a tool to set the foundations of long lasting relationships as well as key to successful project execution. We exist to make delivery of furniture to cruise ships an exceptionally smooth experience.

Reliability

Nothing counts but the results. The owners and architects must be able to rely our results in getting cruise quality design furniture delivered in time and budget. That is our single most important commitment to our clients.
